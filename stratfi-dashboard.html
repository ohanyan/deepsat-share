1. Project Charter

Mission

Close the STRATFI window on 2026-05-29 with the strongest possible package: a funded 1-1-2 match, a DoW end-user commitment, and government letters that unambiguously tie DeepSat's VLEO capability to a named defense need.

In scope

  • STRATFI package authoring, red-team review, and submission
  • Matching capital (equity + venture debt + public match) in writing
  • Government letters of support: SSC, AFRL, NASA/GSFC, DARPA backup
  • SOF Week / SOFWERX Tech Tuesday, SOCOM PEO relationship-building
  • Compliance readiness (mid-May gate)
  • Narrative alignment across PMOD, government deck, DoW investor materials

Out of scope

  • No-bid RFPs (DEE-448 Threat-Aware Autonomy, DEE-450 Scene Construction)
  • Post-submission hiring plan, long-cycle SOCOM maritime exercise prep (post-submission)
  • Product strategy reopening or repositioning during the window
  • Any proposal or activity that does not advance submission, match, or letters

Success criteria

  • Package submitted on or before 2026-05-29, draft-complete by 2026-05-15, red-team pass by 2026-05-18
  • Match in writing by 2026-05-22: Vahe equity, Leonid venture debt up to 75%, at least one public-money source
  • Government letters: SSC LOS signed by 2026-05-03, NASA/GSFC and AFRL by 2026-05-20
  • At least one SOCOM PEO champion identified out of SOF Week
  • Compliance readiness complete by mid-May 2026

Top risks

  • SSC letter not secured in 2 weeks (owner: Nerses, mitigated by parallel paths)
  • Matching capital slips, breaks STRATFI math (owner: Nerses + Subbu)
  • Ryan overloaded on compliance + submission (owner: Ryan + Nerses)
  • Technical volume late for red-team pass (owner: Hayk + consultant)
  • Eureka field window absorbs STRATFI desk bandwidth (owner: CEO delegate)

Constraints

  • Cash: match must come from committed capital, not aspirational
  • Calendar: SOF Week May 18-21 and AFRL Wright-Patt May 13 compete for leadership time
  • Eureka field window Apr 24 - May 2 pulls Vrezh, Eric, Ryan, Nerses, Kostya
  • Compliance readiness is non-negotiable before submission

  • Tue 14:00 PT BD Meeting · existing · Subbu + Sarkis + Nerses · proposal pipeline + partnerships focus
  • Thu 14:30 PT Sarkis/Nerses 1:1 · existing · SOFWERX + SOF Week + BD tactical
  • Fri 16:00 PT STRATFI End-of-Week Retro · 30 min · What shipped, what slipped, what's at risk Monday. Async OK if nothing is red.

Operating principles

  • Single source of truth: SF STRATFI Linear project. If it is not a ticket, it does not exist.
  • Every blocker has an owner and a date. No floating TODOs.
  • Anti-goals are enforced at Weekly Review. No new scope inside the submission window.
  • Async > meetings for status. Meetings only for decisions.

9. Escalation

  • Submission date slips → Nerses + Subbu convene inside 24 hours. Decision: accept slip or cut scope to hold the date.
  • Match gap > 20% unresolved at W4 → DARPA backup path activated, public-money outreach doubled.
  • Compliance blocked → external counsel engaged the same day.
  • Technical volume not draft-complete by W4 → reviewer booked earlier, scope trimmed to core capability.
  • Key-person illness or departure → Nerses assumes Accountable on the affected workstream; Subbu backstops.
