Verification note — 25.4 BAA D2P2 vs prior CSO D2P2 differences

The prior DeepSat D2P2 win (Orion's Belt, F2D-16348) was submitted under the DAF X25.5 CSO (AFWERX/SpaceWERX monthly Open Topic). This submission is under the DoD SBIR 25.4 Annual BAA, Release 12, with DAF Component Instructions v5. The two solicitation vehicles have materially different rules. Verify this table with Kristina Borthelo (spelling TBC) before we rely on it — she's our reference for AFWERX/DSIP process knowledge.
